eSlams · the mobile app · v3 · interactive prototype

v3 — visualization for real, themes for real.

v2 plus the v3 change request. Themes are materials now, not palette swaps — a per-theme material sheet (plane, ink, card, glow), the mark re-tinting to the theme ink in-app, themed alternate app icons in Settings. The win curtain fires theme-aware physics confetti (reduced-motion → accent sweep only); Connect Four drops with weight, stones land with overshoot, the result pushes in like a camera. Arena is one always-expanded 38-game shelf with category dividers and a real opponent module driven by the platform roster (per-game allowedGames availability, nearest-model fallback). Battlefield trades the trust line for Request a match; the recruit card is one line. Humans vs Models now leads with the human ladder (Humans / Models / Everyone lenses, ranked by verified wins). The reasoning ledger reads as a dialogue — model chip once, player-language actions, reasoning as the body. Offline kit and the notification sound are spec’d beside the phone.
